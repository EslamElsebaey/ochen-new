Ochen Elevators was established in 1997 and has since risen to become one of the leading companies in the field of elevator and escalator installation and maintenance in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Our company has more than 27 years of experience and excellence in supplying, installing and maintaining high-quality elevators for various uses in the public and private sectors. We are proud to provide highly efficient lifting and pulling elevators, whether with or without a machine room, which are characterized by safety in movement. Our prompt and regular maintenance services ensure the continuity and efficiency of the elevators at the highest level, ensuring the safety and comfort of our customers. ochen Elevators has obtained all the required certificates and licenses to practice the profession from the accredited authorities, which facilitates our work in all public and private sectors. ochen Elevators is also distinguished by its commitment to the highest standards of quality and safety, and its ability to provide solutions for various elevators for passengers and goods, based on the latest specifications and technologies. In addition, our technical team is distinguished by its efficiency and professionalism in planning and implementing projects at the highest levels.
